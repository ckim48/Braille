Introducing BraillePlay

This project focuses on creating a 2x4 Braille input device and an interactive web platform to make Braille learning accessible and engaging for non-disabled students. By combining tactile hardware with digital content, it bridges the gap between visually impaired and sighted individuals.

  • Hands-on practice with a custom Braille input device.
  • Step-by-step Braille lessons with interactive exercises.
  • Engaging games to reinforce learning and make it fun.
  • Progress tracking with scores and achievements.

Why It Matters

Braille is an essential communication tool for visually impaired individuals, yet it remains unfamiliar to most sighted people. This lack of understanding creates barriers to inclusion and accessibility.

  • Promotes awareness and empathy through hands-on learning.
  • Builds a bridge between sighted and visually impaired communities.
  • Encourages inclusive education with fun and interactive tools.

By engaging students in a meaningful way, this project helps foster a more inclusive society where everyone can appreciate the importance of accessible communication.
